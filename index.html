Superbikes-The Speed Demons
Superbikes are high-performance motorcycles designed for exceptional speed and handling. They feature powerful engines, advanced suspension systems, aerodynamic bodywork, and cutting-edge technology, making them the ultimate choice for thrill-seeking riders.
SUZUKI
Suzuki Hayabusa Gen-3: The Suzuki Hayabusa is an iconic sportbike, known for its extreme speed and sleek design. With a powerful engine of 1340cc that produces 197 hp , 0-300 kmph in 22 sec.
KAWASAKI
Kawasaki ZX-10R: The ZX-10R is a high-performance sportbike by Kawasaki, known for its powerful inline-four engine of 998cc producing 203ps , agile handling, advanced electronics, and aerodynamic design, 0-200 kmph in 7.4s.
APRILIA
Aprilia RSV4 Factory: The Aprilia RSV4 is a super bike manufactured by Aprilia. The RSV4 is Aprilia's flagship model.It has an air-cooled engine that produces 216 PS @ 13000 rpm of with a top speed of 320kmph.

Yamaha R1: Yamaha YZF R1 is a flagship supersport motorcycle, available in 1 variant in India. YZF R1 generates 194.3 bhp @ 13,500 rpm of power and 112.4 Nm @ 11,500 rpm of torque from its 998 cc, 4-cylinder, 4-stroke, 4-valves, liquid-cooled, DOHC engine witj a top speed of 302 kmph.
DUCATIs
